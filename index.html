Explore Our Products!

Whether you're looking for traditional corn tortillas or something unique to fit your needs, Mi-Rancho Tortillas has that great flavor you're looking for.

We produce a variety of products through our two brands, Mi-Rancho and Mi-Familia. We also have the capacity to make custom orders to fill whatever your nee may be.

Most of our products can be found at your local grocery store but we also invite you to come into our retail location where you can purchase our products and much more, all while enjoying a little taste of Mexico!

    The Cruz Family welcomes you to "Mi-Tiendita" which translates to "my little store". Located in the heart of Clovis, CA, "Mi-Tiendita" carries authentic Hispanic products that have been hand selected for your home cooking needs. Anywhere from FRESH flour and Corn tortillas, to regular and prepared masa for tamales.

    Also available are hand crafted artisan imports, direct from Mexico. We personally selected these items to create an experience of nostalgia, just like what your grandparents grew up with. Items that bring back memories of what your grandma used to prepare meals for her family.
